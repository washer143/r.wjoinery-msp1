About Us

Experience

I started my journey as a joiner/carpenter 13 years ago, i was lucky enough to begin an apprenticeship and achieve a City and Guilds qualification in Joinery. I had the pleasure of working with many experienced joiners, and learnt my skills off some of the most talented craftsmen over many years, plying my trade initially in stair making, and then eventually moving on to cabinetry, doors and window making. I picked up the skills and tools necessary, to fabricate all forms of joinery with a high level of skill and attention to detail.

The Workshop

After 7 years of experience, with enough confidence and knowledge, i decided it was time to move on and begin my own long term dream of building my own successful buisness, and that began with getting my own workshop. For the last 5 years we've been situated on 1 white city road and we've built a fully functional workshop with outstanding equipment to help build beautiful high-quality joinery products, this what i strive for, not only for the customers, but for myself.
